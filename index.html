I haven't followed the conventional path to the world of web, app, and software development.
The first half of my life was spent working doubles behind a bar and raising my sons as a single mother. In 2019 I decided it was time for a change, and I enrolled in the Computer Software Engineering program at Thaddeus Stevens College of Technology. There I discovered a new field to not only challenge my problem solving skills, but ignite my creativity and desire for learning as well.
During those years as a bartender, I met, spoke with, and came to know and appreciate people from every walk of life.

I have learned the best ideas can come from the most unexpected places. Diversity and adverisity are provenance for new perspectives and creative soulutions.
